About

What is Historale?

Historale is a free trivia game that tests whether you can tell real historical events from convincing fiction. The real events sound completely made up, and the fake ones sound totally plausible. One wrong answer ends your streak.

How to Play

You're shown a historical "fact" and must decide: did this actually happen, or is it fabricated? Tap Real or Fake. Get it right and your streak continues. Get it wrong and it's game over. After each answer, you'll get a detailed explanation of why it's real or fake.
