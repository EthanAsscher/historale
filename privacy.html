Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Historale is a free browser-based trivia game. We respect your privacy and collect minimal data.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. We do not require registration, login, or an email address to play.

Your game progress (streak, best score, accuracy) is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand how many people visit the site and which pages are popular. This data is aggregated and anonymous ‚Äî we cannot identify individual visitors.

Advertising

We may display advertisements through third-party ad networks such as Google AdSense or Mediavine. These services may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can learn more about Google's ad practices at Google's advertising policies page.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

Cookies

Historale itself does not set cookies. However, third-party advertising partners may set cookies for ad delivery and measurement. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

Historale is a general-audience trivia game. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
